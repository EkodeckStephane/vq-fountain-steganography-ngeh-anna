Highlights

* Coverless VQ steganography recovers payloads without auxiliary indexes.
* Fountain coding enables exact 64-byte and 128-byte hard-channel recovery.
* Ablations isolate the roles of projection binning, anchors, and scheduling.
* Passive evidence is reported as detector-bounded, not as a security proof.
* SOTA claims are separated by capacity, auxiliary channel, and robustness.
